Unparalleled Data and Delivery

  • We deliver ads designed to maintain flow

    No one likes to be interrupted, especially in moments of deep thought or focus. Traditional ads either share screenspace with applications as you use them, or are delivered as notifications that directly interrupt you. Unlike on a desktop or laptop, mobile applications do not have a surplus of screenspace to share with advertisements. When one appears, it interferes directly with whatever the user is paying attention too.


    We do things differently. Our ads appear to the user in periods of waiting, and do so for short periods of time. We use those moments to their fullest extent by presenting advertisements in only one format: as fullscreen, rich and optionally interactive experiences.

  • Track your ads across our network in real time

    Using our desktop and mobile interfaces, advertisers can view metrics, set alerts, view suggestions on improving their creatives, and watch as their changes propagate instantly.

    We focus heavily on user and application behaviour, delivering ads optimized for the expected time available for display. With the option to publish multi-scene advertisements, you can track user engagement per scene and optimize accordingly.

Build and Preview Ads in the Cloud

  • Design and create ads in a familiar actor-based workflow.

    Ads behave identically on mobiles and in the browser. Build experiences for your users and rigorously test them before deployment. Select from a wide range of stock effects and animations, while getting valuable feedback on optimal devices and potential improvements.
